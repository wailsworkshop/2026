# The Workshop 
The **3rd Workshop on Artificial Intelligence with and for Learning Sciences: Past, Present, and Future Horizons** (WAILS 2026) is a high-quality forum focused on achievements, challenges, solutions, and future perspectives concerned with the adoption of **artificial intelligence** methods and techniques in the context of **learning sciences**. Building on its successful previous editions, WAILS 2026 brings together researchers and practitioners working within academia, industry, and government, and coming from different fields such as computer science, education, cognitive science, economics, psychology, sociology, and human-computer interaction, with the goal of fostering a fruitful **dialogue across disciplines**. The accepted papers are going to be published by **Springer** into a **post-workshop proceedings volume** of the Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series, and submitted for indexing to **DBLP**, **Google Scholar**, and **Scopus**.

# City and Region

## Paris  
**Paris**, the capital of **France**, is a city that seamlessly blends **history**, **art**, and **innovation**. Famous for its **elegant boulevards**, **world-class museums**, and **iconic landmarks** such as the **Eiffel Tower** and **Notre-Dame**, Paris embodies a timeless charm that continues to inspire visitors from around the world. Along its lively streets and riverside quays, one can feel the city’s **creative pulse**, where centuries of culture meet modern design and cosmopolitan life. With its **refined cuisine** and **vibrant atmosphere**, Paris remains a destination **for all seasons**.

## Île-de-France  
**Île-de-France**, the region surrounding **Paris**, stands as the **cultural and economic heart of France**. It encompasses a remarkable variety of **landscapes**—from **royal palaces** and **lush forests** to **modern business hubs** and **historic towns**. Home to treasures such as **Versailles** and **Fontainebleau**, the region reflects the **grandeur and diversity** of French heritage. Beyond its renowned monuments, Île-de-France also offers **peaceful countryside escapes**, **dynamic universities**, and **thriving artistic communities**, making it a place where **tradition and progress** harmoniously coexist.
